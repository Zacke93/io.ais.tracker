Never miss a bridge opening on the Trollhätte canal again.

AIS Bridge tracks vessels on the Trollhätte canal in Trollhättan, Sweden, in real time using live AIS data, and tells you when the Klaffbron or Stridsbergsbron bridge is about to open.

The app gives you a Bridge status device with a constantly updated status text, for example "One boat heading towards Klaffbron, estimated bridge opening in 5 minutes". The status text is perfect for dashboards, and the device also shows the AIS connection status and a generic alarm that turns on whenever a boat is approaching one of the two opening bridges.

With the flow card "Boat near bridge" you can trigger any automation the moment a vessel comes close to a bridge of your choice, from the canal entrance in the south to Stallbackabron in the north. The trigger provides the vessel name, the bridge, the sailing direction and the estimated time to arrival, so you can send notifications, switch lights, or have your speaker announce the next bridge opening. A condition card lets your flows check whether a boat is currently at a bridge.

Estimated arrival times are calculated from each vessel's position, speed and course, with smart filtering that handles anchored boats, waiting boats and noisy AIS data, so you only get notified when it matters.

To use the app you need a free API key from aisstream.io. Create an account at https://aisstream.io, copy your key into the app settings, and the app connects automatically.
